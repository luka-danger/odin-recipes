Taco Bowl

Taco 'bout a high protein meal, bro!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of TVP (Textured Vegetable Protein)
  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • 1/2 can of black beans (Drained)
  • 1/4 small yellow onion
  • 1 cup of raw spinach
  • 1 bell pepper (Your choice in color)
  • 1 small avocado
  • 1 tsp of olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Pour TVP in a bowl. Add 1/4 of the boiling water into the bowl with TVP and let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Fill the rest of the pot of boiling water with your cup of brown rice. Lower the heat to medium and cover for 10 minutes.
  4. Once most of the water is out of pot and bowl, combine the brown rice and TVP together in the pot and add your spices. Stir and cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Cook your half can of black beans on medium heat for 5 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  6. Chop your onion and bell pepper. Add in a heated pan with 1 tsp of olive oil. onions have browned.
  7. In a bowl, add 1 cup of spinach. Add TVP, brown rice, beans, and vegetables. Top with sliced avocado and sauce of your choice.
